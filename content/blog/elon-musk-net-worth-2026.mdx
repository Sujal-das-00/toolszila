## The Tax Question: How Much Does He Actually Keep?

Here's a question nobody asks: **How much of that wealth actually goes to taxes?**

**The answer:** Surprisingly little, thanks to legal tax strategies:

- **Unrealized gains:** No tax until sold
- **Margin loans:** Borrow against stock without selling (and tax implications)
- **Asset appreciation:** Only taxed when liquidated
- **Corporate structures:** Optimize through legal holding companies

**Current estimate:** Elon has paid ~$11 billion in taxes over his entire lifetime despite being worth $820 billion. That's a **0.014% effective tax rate**.
